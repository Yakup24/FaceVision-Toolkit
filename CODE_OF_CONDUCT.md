# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.
- Avoid sharing private camera data, real face images or sensitive logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private images, videos or personal data.
- Pressuring maintainers to review unsafe or privacy-invasive changes.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Public issues should not contain private media, personal data or security-sensitive details.
